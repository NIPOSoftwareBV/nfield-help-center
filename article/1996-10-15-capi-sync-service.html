Network Service Interruptions

Date: Tue Oct 15 1996.
Tags:

ISSUE REPORTED

1989-06-11 10:15 CET
A small number of customers in the AM region are reporting major service interruptions.

NIPO SOFTWARE INVESTIGATION

Support Engineers are attempting to reproduce and resolve the syncing interruptions.

STATUS UPDATE

1989-06-11 12:32 CET
Support Engineers have determined that the interruptions were caused by extensive solar flares that momentarily meddled with the ionosphere.

There is no lasting effect to service interruptions at this time.
